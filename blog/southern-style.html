Unique Flavors and Southern Charm

At Wingz and Thingz, it's not just about the foodâ€”it's about the unique flavors and southern hospitality that set us apart. Every dish on our menu has a story and a carefully crafted flavor profile that keeps our customers coming back for more. Let's take a closer look at what makes Wingz and Thingz truly special.


The OMG Wings: A Culinary Masterpiece

One of our standout offerings is the OMG Wings, a creation by Anthony's wife that has become a sensation. These wings are not just another menu item; they're an experience. Each bite delivers a burst of flavors that dance on your palate, leaving you wanting more. The secret blend of spices and the perfect balance of heat and sweetness make these wings an unforgettable treat. The OMG Wings are a testament to our commitment to creativity and culinary excellence.


Garlic Fries: Silky, Soft Perfection

Our garlic fries are another customer favorite. These fries are not just seasoned; they are elevated to a whole new level of deliciousness. The silky, soft flavor of the garlic pairs perfectly with the crispy texture of the fries. Each fry is a delightful combination of crunch and flavor that's hard to resist. They're the perfect side to any meal or a satisfying snack on their own.


Sweet Chili Tenders: Glossy and Irresistible

The sweet chili tenders at Wingz and Thingz are a visual and culinary delight. Coated in a glossy sweet chili sauce, these tenders are both sweet and spicy, creating a harmonious blend of flavors. The sauce is sticky and rich, clinging to the tender chicken in a way that makes every bite a burst of flavor. It's this attention to detail and flavor that makes our sweet chili tenders a standout dish.


Buffalo Fries: Local Favorite

Our buffalo fries are a local favorite, and it's easy to see why. These fries have the perfect crunch on the outside and a soft, tender inside. Tossed in our signature buffalo sauce, they're a spicy, tangy delight that's hard to beat. The balance of textures and flavors makes them an addictive treat that our customers can't get enough of. Whether as a side or a main dish, buffalo fries never disappoint.


Southern Style Lemonade: A Refreshing Delight

To wash down all this delicious food, our Southern style lemonade is the perfect choice. This refreshing beverage is a great way to quench your thirst, especially after enjoying our spicier dishes. Made with fresh lemons and just the right amount of sweetness, it's a delightful drink that complements our menu perfectly. It's another example of our commitment to southern hospitality and flavor.


Traditional Southern Cooking: Crisp and Crunchy

At Wingz and Thingz, we pride ourselves on cooking the traditional way of the south. This means ensuring that our food is nice, crunchy, and crispy. We achieve this by frying with no batter, a technique that allows the natural flavors of the ingredients to shine through while maintaining a delightful crunch. This method of cooking is a hallmark of southern cuisine and is part of what makes our food so special.


A Flavorful Experience

What truly sets Wingz and Thingz apart is our dedication to unique flavors and traditional southern cooking. From the innovative OMG wings to the perfectly seasoned garlic fries and the glossy sweet chili tenders, every item on our menu is crafted with care and passion. Our buffalo fries and southern style lemonade add to the experience, making every visit to Wingz and Thingz a delightful culinary journey. We invite you to come and experience the unique flavors and southern charm that make Wingz and Thingz special. Whether you're a first-time visitor or a loyal customer, we're sure you'll find something to love on our menu. So come on down, grab a plate, and savor the flavors that make us the best in town.
